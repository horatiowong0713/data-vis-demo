CDC COVID-19 Map

https://covid.cdc.gov/covid-data-tracker/#county-view

Summary: The visualization is about the seriousness of COVID-19 in AZ state. There are five different scales, namely High(red), Substantial(orange), Moderate(yellow), Low(blue) and No Data(grey).

Domain: This is a geometry domain providing spreading of severity of COVID-19 in the whole state.

Data:The data are about the change of cases that people are diagnosed with positive test from previous 7-days (8/17 - 8/23) to current 7-days (8/19 - 8/25). The percent change in counties at each level of transmission is the absolute change compared to the previous 7-day period.

Task:This map is useful for governmental agency, medical workers and other related staff to monitor the status of COVID-19.

Idiom: This map is divided into several blocks, and is able to be zoomed in. People are able to track any changes and statistics in each block area.

Personal thoughts: This is a good visualization because it provides precise and direct information telling the degree of severity COVID-19 cases in AZ. People will easily comprehend this information.
