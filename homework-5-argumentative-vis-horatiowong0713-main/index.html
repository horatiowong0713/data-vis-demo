Tokyo 2020 Olympic Games

Tokyo 2020 Summer Olympic Games was finally launched in summer 2021. However, the launch of Tokyo games raised a lot of controversies since cumulative number of infected patients by COVID-19 in Japan was reported to be over 160K. While most Japanese nations were against Olympic games, the Japanese government still insisted on it. Therefore, we investigate results of modern Olympic games from 2000 to 2021 to argue whether Tokyo Omlypic Games is worthy for Japan.

Opposive Argument:
Tokyo 2020 Olympic is worthless.


This chart indicates the number of new atheletes in each games. We can observe that the number of new atheletes attending Tokyo 2020 (3833) is significantly lower than those in Rio 2016 (4111), London 2012 (3914) and Beijing 2008 (3914). This phenominon reflects the fact that many atheletes around the world hestitated to attend Tokyo 2020 due to higher number of COVID-19 cases in Japan. In this sense, Tokyo 2020 is worthless for Japan since fewer new althetes attended.


Visual rhetoric techniques:
Information Access Rhetoric, Provenance Rhetoric, Mapping Rhetoric, Linguistic-based Rhetoric, Procedural Rhetoric, Patterns of Occurrence

Supportive Argument:
Tokyo 2020 Olympic is worthy.


This chart indicates the number of medals received by Japanese team. We can observe that the number of medals Japanese team won significantly increases, especifically the number of golden models. Note that Japanese team won 28 gold medals in Tokyo 2020, more than those in Rio 2016 (13), London 2012 (7), Beijing 2008 (9), Athens 2004 (16) and Sydney 2000 (5). Since Japanese team made outbreaking records in the earned medals, Tokyo 2020 is a success for Japan.


Visual rhetoric techniques:
Information Access Rhetoric, Provenance Rhetoric, Mapping Rhetoric, Linguistic-based Rhetoric, Procedural Rhetoric, Patterns of Occurrence

Data reference: Olympic Games, 1986-2021
